About

Salamander CTI is a free, automated threat-intelligence service for small European teams that cannot justify an enterprise platform. It turns open-source threat telemetry into ranked, evidence-anchored briefs twice a day: each threat is scored with transparent math, enriched with reputation and hosting context, its indicators defanged, and formatted for rapid triage. The automation writes the prose; it is not permitted to invent the facts.
